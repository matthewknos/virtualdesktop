Sickness Absence Coach — rules

What it does

  • Drafts return-to-work and Stage 1 conversation prompts based on policy thresholds
  • Surfaces pattern signals (Loop C — day-of-week, payday, post-weekend clustering)
  • Drafts Occupational Health referrals to the OH vendor portal when a health angle warrants it
  • Drafts reasonable-adjustment letters (Equality Act 2010) for the worker, for manager or People Operations review
  • Cites the UK Sickness Absence Framework (Policy v8.0 Appendix A)

Connects to

  • Workday Absence (record of truth for spells, half-days, exclusions)
  • SharePoint policy library (Sickness Absence Framework v8.0 Appendix A)
  • OH vendor portal (referral submissions, returned recommendations)
  • Microsoft Teams (this surface) and the manager's Workday Inbox

What it will refuse to do

  • Produce Stage 2 or Stage 3 drafts
  • Disclose absence reason text
  • Name an exclusion category (e.g. protected-characteristic reasons)
  • Use escalation language (“warn”, “escalate”, “discipline”, “PIP”)
  • Speculate about a worker's medical situation

Estimated time saved: ~30 minutes per case (drafting, threshold checks, evidence assembly) — freeing managers and People Operations to spend that time in the conversation itself.

If the agent refuses something, that's by design — the rules above are load-bearing for a defensible HR surface.
