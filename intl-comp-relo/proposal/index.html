What the agent does.

  1. 01Reads the requisition, resolves the subject’s home and destination context, and runs a freshness check across all six source-of-truth feeds before drafting anything.
  2. 02Drafts a full package into a Teams adaptive card for Total Rewards — band positioning, FX-normalised base, COL delta, relocation policy, benefits parity, equal-pay justification field.
  3. 03Classifies the tax case. Routine (UK↔IE PAYE gross-up, UK↔PL PIT) runs through a versioned rule engine. Non-routine flags to the external tax advisor with the case attached. The agent never invents a tax position.
  4. 04Logs every recommendation against UK Equality Act 2010, Irish Employment Equality Acts, German Entgelttransparenzgesetz, and the EU Pay Transparency Directive — band position, justification, inputs snapshot, approver. 7-year retention.
  5. 05Refuses to produce a package if any input is stale (FX > 30 days, COL > 90 days, bands > 1 quarter). Stops upstream rot from leaking into an offer.

What could go wrong, and how we contain it.

Equal-pay risk is the headline. Tax non-routine and stale data are the operational risks.

Risk A cross-border recommendation isn’t defensible against UK Equality Act 2010, Irish Employment Equality Acts, German Entgelttransparenzgesetz, or the EU Pay Transparency Directive.
Mitigation Every package logs band position, justification (mandatory outside the 25th–75th percentile), inputs snapshot, approver. 7-year retention. Quarterly fairness review with HRD + CFO + Legal against the underlying source versions.
Risk The agent invents a tax position on a non-routine case (equity vesting cross-border, dual citizenship, US inbound).
Mitigation Routine cases only via a versioned rule engine (UK↔IE PAYE gross-up, UK↔PL PIT) signed off annually by the external tax advisor. Non-routine cases hard-flag to the advisor with the case attached; the agent stops calculating.
Risk Stale source data corrupts an offer — FX two months old, COL a year stale, a band re-published mid-cycle.
Mitigation Single freshness regime: FX 30 days, COL 90 days, bands and policy quarterly, tax rules annual re-confirm. Any stale input → full refusal to draft, surfaced to TR with the offending field.
Risk The hiring manager sees an internal band, percentile, or justification text they shouldn’t.
Mitigation Confidentiality masking on every hiring-manager-audience message. Total Rewards, HRD, CFO see everything. Hiring manager sees the package output only. Tested in the eval harness against a regex of leakable terms.

Three phases. The data work is the lift.

Upstream — do regardless
Consolidate bands, FX, COL, relocation matrix, benefits parity rules, and routine tax-equalisation rules into a single Workday-resident source of truth. ~6 weeks integration, sponsored by Total Rewards lead + Workday IT. Runs in parallel to the agent build.
Pragmatic — v1
UK↔IE + UK↔PL drafter. Agent reads, drafts the package into a Teams adaptive card, logs the justification. Total Rewards reviews, edits, approves, or refers to the external advisor. TA still owns the candidate conversation. 10 weeks, ~1.9 FTE.
Bold — v2
Total Rewards Co-pilot. Agent owns the package end-to-end, posts to the approval queue, then to the candidate with TA in the loop only on negotiation. Full jurisdictional coverage. Pairs with Workday Talent Mobility — TM surfaces the move, this agent prices it.

Workday Compensation and Benefits are read-only inputs in v1. Workday Talent Mobility (announced) pairs in v2. No portfolio overlap blocks v1.
