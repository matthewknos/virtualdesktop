R&R Auto-Approver

An agent that auto-approves the roughly 190 in-policy Reward & Recognition nominations each cycle directly in Workday, and routes the ~10 genuine exceptions to People Ops through the existing business process. Every decision — approval or escalation — lands in a SharePoint Excel sheet / Workday worksheet, so the whole team can see what the agent did and why without opening Workday.
